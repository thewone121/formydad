The Firm

A commercial practice built on principle, precision and permanence.

Since 2001, Pentium Chambers has advised the corporations, banks and institutions that shape Nigeria's economy — combining the conviction of a litigation-led firm with the discipline of a modern, technology-driven practice.

Our History

01 — Since 2001

Duly registered at the Corporate Affairs Commission in Abuja, Pentium Chambers has been in active legal practice for more than two decades.

The firm was founded on committed professional ideals, and has spent over twenty years in extensive practice across many fields of law. Over the years, it has enlarged its activities to include general litigation together with commercial, corporate and kindred institutional practice — representing corporate bodies, individuals and statutory corporations.

With its main office in Abuja, and through an extensive network of associates in Nigeria and beyond, the firm is particularly well placed to render legal services anywhere in the country and abroad.
