Expertise

Twelve disciplines. One standard of counsel.

Litigation forms the core of the firm's practice — but our counsel reaches across the commercial, corporate and institutional matters that define modern business in Nigeria. Select an area to explore how we can help.

Litigation is the heart of Pentium Chambers. We represent corporate bodies, individuals and statutory corporations in complex commercial disputes — from contract and debt-recovery actions to injunctions and appellate advocacy — bringing conviction, strategy and courtroom command to every brief.

  • Contract & commercial disputes
  • Debt recovery & enforcement
  • Injunctions & interim relief
  • Shareholder & partnership disputes
  • Appellate advocacy
  • Freezing & asset-preservation orders

We guide companies from formation to maturity — advising on structure, governance and the everyday legal machinery of running a business. Our corporate practice pairs commercial pragmatism with rigorous attention to compliance.

  • Company incorporation & CAC filings
  • Corporate governance & compliance
  • Mergers, acquisitions & restructuring
  • Shareholder & joint-venture agreements
  • Board & secretarial advisory
  • Regulatory approvals & licensing

A cornerstone of the firm. We act for banks, financial institutions and their customers across the full lifecycle of lending and security — and bring particular depth in receivership and the recovery of secured assets. Our clients have included leading institutions such as GTBank, Polaris Bank and the Chartered Institute of Bankers.

  • Loan & facility documentation
  • Security creation & perfection
  • Receivership & asset recovery
  • Banking regulation & compliance
  • Debt restructuring
  • Enforcement of financial claims

We represent clients in the full spectrum of civil disputes before the courts — protecting rights, property and commercial interests with disciplined advocacy and careful case strategy.

  • Property & land disputes
  • Tort & negligence claims
  • Contractual claims
  • Estate & succession matters
  • Enforcement of judgments
  • Injunctive relief

Our advocates defend individuals and corporate officers in criminal proceedings, with particular experience in economic and financial crime. We combine forceful representation with meticulous preparation and an unwavering commitment to due process.

  • White-collar & economic crime
  • Fraud & financial-offence defence
  • Corporate criminal liability
  • Bail & pre-trial applications
  • Regulatory & investigative response
  • Appeals

We handle constitutional and public-law matters of significance — from the enforcement of fundamental rights to the judicial review of administrative action — advocating with rigour on questions that shape the boundaries of state and citizen.

  • Fundamental-rights enforcement
  • Judicial review
  • Administrative-law challenges
  • Election & public-interest matters
  • Statutory interpretation
  • Government & regulatory disputes

We advise across the energy value chain, combining transactional capability with contentious experience — from licensing and joint ventures to sector disputes and regulatory engagement.

  • Licensing & concessions
  • Joint-venture & farm-in agreements
  • Energy contracts
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Sector dispute resolution
  • Local-content advisory

Our arbitration department deals with a wide range of arbitral settlements. Where litigation is not the right path, we help clients resolve disputes efficiently and confidentially through arbitration, mediation and negotiated settlement.

  • Institutional & ad-hoc arbitration
  • Commercial mediation
  • Arbitral settlement & awards
  • Enforcement & setting-aside
  • Drafting arbitration clauses
  • Negotiated dispute resolution

As emerging markets in artificial intelligence, information technology and banking reshape Nigeria, we counsel clients at the intersection of law and technology — bringing a modern, forward-looking perspective to a fast-moving field.

  • Data protection & privacy
  • Artificial-intelligence governance
  • Fintech & digital-payments advisory
  • Technology & licensing contracts
  • Intellectual property
  • Cyber & regulatory compliance

Drawing on the Principal Partner's experience in capital-market operations, we advise on securities transactions, market operations and the regulatory framework that governs them.

  • Securities issuance & offerings
  • Market-operation advisory
  • Regulatory filings & approvals
  • Investor & issuer counsel
  • Compliance & disclosure
  • Market-conduct matters

Beyond individual matters, we serve as trusted strategic counsel — helping boards and management anticipate risk, structure transactions and make confident, well-informed decisions.

  • Board & management advisory
  • Transaction structuring
  • Legal risk assessment
  • Regulatory strategy
  • Contract review & negotiation
  • Ongoing outside-counsel retainers

Our consultancy practice draws on deep experience in public-policy formulation, legal drafting and legal research — supporting institutions and organisations with specialist counsel and considered analysis.

  • Public-policy formulation
  • Legislative & legal drafting
  • Legal research & opinions
  • Institutional advisory
  • Compliance frameworks
  • Specialist consultancy services
