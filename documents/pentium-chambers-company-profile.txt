COMPANY PROFILE
------------------------------------------------------------

PENTIUM CHAMBERS is a legal practice firm duly registered at the
Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja since 2001. The firm is engaged
in active legal practice based on committed professional ideals
spanning over two decades of extensive practice in various fields
of law.

With its main office in Abuja, the firm is particularly well placed
to render legal services anywhere in the country and abroad. The
firm has extensive reach and associates in Nigeria and beyond.

------------------------------------------------------------
STATEMENT OF PRACTICE
------------------------------------------------------------

Over the years, the firm has enlarged its activities to include
general litigation, commercial, corporate and kindred institutional
law practice, representing corporate bodies, individuals and
statutory corporations.

Litigation forms the core of the firm's practice. The firm handles
all types of litigation: commercial, constitutional, oil & gas,
corporate, civil and criminal. Our arbitration department also
deals with a wide range of arbitral settlements.

With the prevalence of emerging markets in artificial intelligence,
information technology and banking in Nigeria, and as trends towards
legal globalisation continue, the firm remains committed to
providing highly professional legal service by constantly expanding
the use of technology advancements and investing in its
professional resources. Our bank clients include GTB, Polaris Bank,
Chartered Institute of Bankers, and others.

It is not just the law that binds us together at our firm. It is
also a shared belief in how we should work with our clients to
ensure satisfaction. Whatever we are handling, our commitment to
high-quality work is uncompromising.

We have a strong sense of responsibility, both to the community and
to society as a whole. So we regularly act without profit. We
sometimes act for clients who cannot pay at all. Our goal is, and
will continue to be, the enshrinement of client satisfaction at all
times.

PRINCIPAL PARTNER
